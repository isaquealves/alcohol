SQLAlchemy Support
------------------

In addition to implementations of the :class:`~alcohol.mixins.EmailMixin` and
:class:`~alcohol.mixins.PasswordMixin` classes, the
:mod:`~alcohol.mixins.sqlalchemy` module comes with support for auto-updating
timestamps.
